Welcome to **liminal.cafe**: a collective of programmers, artists, and hobbyists.

LC is an attempt to bring creative minds together to share information, collaborate, and bring back a modicum of the magic that coalesced when the internet was young.

### services

- **note**: these are only available to our users.
  - [bin.liminal](https://bin.liminal.cafe): a temporary file hosting service conducted through the command line.
  - [txt.liminal](https://txt.liminal.cafe): a multi-user blogging platform with federation capabilities.
  - [znc.liminal](https://znc.liminal.cafe): irc bouncer.
